Sunsetting a product

After researching a certain problem, the product team and I realized that users did not need the product we had in it's current form.

We decided we needed to pivot and began to explore ways we could solve the problem. However upon presenting this to executives, the product was killed.

Despite this, we ended up saving the company a lot of money and helped them focus on a more viable business model.

I would rather work on a product that solves a real problem and is sustainable for the company and users.
